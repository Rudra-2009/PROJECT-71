What's new



  • Updated computer lab

    Computer Education is a key in overall growth of a child for living in this rapidly advancing world. Calcutta Boys’ School has two Computer Laboratories which are well designed and equipped as per the requirement of CISCE Curriculum. With the modern hardware and latest Software loaded in the machines in the newly constructed Senior Lab, our students are getting adequate practice on the programming techniques. Our Junior Lab offers Computer Education for the Students of class 1 to 8.
